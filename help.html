🧠 Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I register a skill?
    Go to “Register Skill” from the navbar, fill out your skill and availability, and submit!

  2. Do I need to pay to use TalenTrade?
    No! TalenTrade is completely free for students. It’s all about helping each other grow 🌱

  3. Can I both teach and learn?
    Absolutely! You can register as both a skill sharer and a learner.

  4. How will I know if a match is made?
    Once someone registers a matching skill, it appears in your “Match Skills” page.

  5. Who can I contact for support?
    Reach out to us at support@talentrade.com
